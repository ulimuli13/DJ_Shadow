Joshua Paul
Joshua Paul Davis, born in 1972
in California, is an American DJ and producer. He began experimenting
with turntables and samples
as a teenager, developing a unique
style influenced by funk,
rock, and hip-hop.
Controversial
DJ Shadow’s debut album
Endtroducing..... sparked controversy
due to its heavy use of samples,
raising copyright concerns. While
praised for its creativity, it also
sparked debates about
artistic freedom and the legal limits of sampling in music.
Endtroducing
Released in 1996, Endtroducing.....
is considered the first album made
entirely from samples. DJ Shadow
blended beats, spoken fragments, and musical snippets into a dense,
atmospheric soundscape —
a landmark in
instrumental hip-hop.
